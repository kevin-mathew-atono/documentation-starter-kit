# Timebox

A named, date-bounded container grouping stories and bugs into a defined delivery window. Can contain up to 100 items, appear on multiple timelines, and show warnings when items may not complete by end date. Displayed on timelines with visual indicators for themes, teams, and delivery risks. Detail view shows item-level projected completion and total story points if all items belong to one team.
