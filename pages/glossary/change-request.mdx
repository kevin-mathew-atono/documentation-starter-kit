# Change Request

A formal or informal proposal to modify agreed-upon scope, requirements, or acceptance criteria after work has begun. Change requests can originate from stakeholders, product managers, or the team itself. Accepting a change request mid-sprint typically affects the sprint goal and may require removing other items to maintain realistic scope.
