# Scrum Master

The Scrum team role responsible for facilitating Scrum ceremonies, coaching the team on Scrum practices, and removing impediments that block progress. The Scrum Master is a servant-leader who protects the team from outside distractions, ensures the sprint goal stays visible, and drives continuous improvement through retrospectives. Scrum Master is not a formal permission role in Atono but is typically the team member who manages sprint cadence, maintains the burndown chart, and coordinates with stakeholders during sprint reviews.

## Also Known As

- SM
- Agile Coach
