# Notification

An email message sent to a user when a relevant event occurs in Atono. Notification triggers include: being assigned to a story or bug, being @mentioned in a comment, a comment being accepted or rejected, a story or bug being moved to Done. Notifications are sent to the user's registered email address. Users can adjust which events generate notifications in their account settings.
