# Team Overview (Page)

A team page showing general information about a team including its name, description, methodology (Scrum or Kanban), access type (public or private), members, and stakeholders. Stakeholders — users with a critical team role (Team Administrator or Backlog Owner) who are not team members — appear in a dedicated section for visibility.
