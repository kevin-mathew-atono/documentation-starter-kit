# Cycle Time

The total time a story or bug spends in workflow steps assigned to the In Progress category for a specific team. Cycle time begins when an item first enters an In Progress step and ends when it leaves the last In Progress step (entering Done or Won't do). Items with unusually long cycle times can be marked as outliers to exclude them from averages, staleness calculations, and estimated completion dates.
