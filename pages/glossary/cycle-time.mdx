# Cycle Time

The total time a story or bug spends in workflow steps categorized as In Progress for a specific team. Includes all time in those steps even if the item moves back and forth or leaves temporarily. Resets to zero if the item is reassigned to a different team. Excludes items marked as outliers. Used to calculate estimated completion dates for Kanban teams and track team performance.
