# Story Points

A numerical measure derived from story size (XXS=1, XS=2, S=3, M=5, L=8, XL=13, XXL=21) using Fibonacci numbers. Unsized stories are assigned the nearest Fibonacci number to the team's average. Used for sprint capacity planning, burndown charts, and velocity tracking in Scrum teams.
