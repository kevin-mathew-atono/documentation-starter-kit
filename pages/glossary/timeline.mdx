# Timeline

A visual schedule of timeboxes created and owned by a user, showing how work is organized over time. Supports scrolling, zooming (days to 5 years), filtering by team/product theme, release markers, and timebox management (add, reorder, resize, color, remove). Displays warnings for timeboxes ending within 3 months that have delivery risks.
