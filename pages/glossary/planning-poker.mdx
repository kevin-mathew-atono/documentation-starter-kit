# Planning Poker

A consensus-based estimation technique where team members simultaneously reveal effort estimates for a story using cards or a digital tool. Differences in estimates prompt discussion, surfacing assumptions and risks that might otherwise go unnoticed. After discussion, the team re-estimates until they reach agreement. Planning poker encourages collaborative sizing rather than anchoring to a single person's estimate.

## Also Known As

- Scrum Poker
