# Priority

A relative ranking of work items indicating their order of importance or urgency. In Atono, priority is expressed through backlog ordering: items higher in the backlog queue are expected to be worked on sooner. Teams can also use product themes or labels to signal strategic priority. Backlog owners are responsible for maintaining priority order when backlog management is enabled.
