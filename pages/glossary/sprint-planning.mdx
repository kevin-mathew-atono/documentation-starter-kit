# Sprint Planning

A Scrum ceremony at the start of each sprint where the team selects stories from the backlog and commits to a sprint goal. The team reviews capacity, estimates remaining uncertainty, and agrees on which items can realistically be completed. In Atono, sprint planning is done from the Backlog page and may be restricted to backlog owners when Backlog Management is enabled.
