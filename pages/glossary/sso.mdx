# SSO

Single sign-on, an authentication method allowing users to sign in to Atono using their organization's identity provider instead of email-based login links. Supports SAML 2.0 for workspaces on paid plans. Three modes: On (all users), Off (no users), Testing (Owners and Administrators only).
