# Staleness Indicator

A red hourglass icon appearing on stories or bugs in In Progress steps when time spent exceeds 3 standard deviations of the team's average for similar items in that step. Requires at least 5 historical items for calculation. Excludes outliers. Can be snoozed by team admins and workspace Administrators. Resets when item changes steps or teams.
