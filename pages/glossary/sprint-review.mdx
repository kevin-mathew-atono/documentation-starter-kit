# Sprint Review

A Scrum ceremony held at the end of each sprint where the team demonstrates completed work to stakeholders and gathers feedback. The sprint review provides an opportunity to inspect the increment and adapt the product backlog based on what was learned. It is distinct from the retrospective, which focuses on team process rather than product output.
