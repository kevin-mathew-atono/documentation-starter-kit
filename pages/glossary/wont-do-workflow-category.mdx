# Won't do (Workflow Category)

A workflow category containing one step for work that will not be completed. Items in Won't do do not appear in some views, don't contribute to cycle time, velocity, or burndown charts, and are excluded from staleness indicators. Each workflow must have exactly one Won't do step.
