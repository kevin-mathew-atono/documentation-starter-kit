# Backlog Management

A team setting that, when enabled, assigns backlog owners responsibility for managing upcoming stories including adding, creating, reordering in To do, deleting, and moving in/out of Won't do. For Scrum teams, also controls sprint planning permissions. When disabled, anyone with team access can manage stories.
