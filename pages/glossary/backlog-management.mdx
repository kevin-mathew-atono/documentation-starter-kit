# Backlog Management

A team setting that, when enabled, designates specific backlog owners as the only users authorized to add, create, reorder, delete, or move stories in the To do and Won't do workflow categories. For Scrum teams, backlog management also controls who can plan and modify sprint scope. When disabled, any user with team access can manage the backlog freely. Up to 10 backlog owners can be assigned to a team.
