# Comment

A discussion thread on a story or bug, either on the item as a whole or anchored to specific highlighted text or an acceptance criterion. Supports @mentions (which trigger notifications), inline item ID references, and per-comment actions including accept, reject, edit, and delete. Open comments appear in a side panel; resolved comments are archived in Activities.
