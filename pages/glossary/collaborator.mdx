# Collaborator

A user who contributes to the work on a story or bug without being the primary assignee. Collaborators may comment, update acceptance criteria, add attachments, or move the item through workflow steps. In Atono, any team member with access to a team's stories can act as a collaborator. The assignee field tracks primary ownership while collaboration is open to all team members.
