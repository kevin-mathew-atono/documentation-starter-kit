# Board View

A visual display of work items organized in vertical columns, one per workflow step, allowing teams to see the state of all active work at a glance. Items can be dragged between columns to update their workflow step. Board view is the primary view on the In Progress page in Atono and supports filtering by assignee, size, and other fields.
