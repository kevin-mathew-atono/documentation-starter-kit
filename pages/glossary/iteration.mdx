# Iteration

A fixed-length time-boxed development cycle used to deliver a defined set of work. In Scrum, iterations are called sprints. In other agile frameworks, iteration length and structure vary. The term is often used interchangeably with sprint in Atono contexts.

## Also Known As

- Development Iteration
