# Watcher

A user who follows a story or bug to receive notifications about changes to that item without being the primary assignee. In Atono, following an item (via the star icon) acts as the watch mechanism, causing the item to appear in the user's followed items widget and personal filters. This is called Follow in Atono.

## Also Known As

- Follower
- Subscriber
