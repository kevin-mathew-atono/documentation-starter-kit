# Acceptance Rate

The percentage of stories or acceptance criteria that pass review on the first attempt without requiring rework or rejection. A high acceptance rate indicates that stories are well-defined before development begins and that implementation matches what was specified. Teams track acceptance rate as a quality indicator, often pairing it with cycle time to understand both speed and quality of delivery.

## Also Known As

- First-Pass Acceptance Rate
