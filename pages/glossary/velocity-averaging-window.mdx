# Velocity Averaging Window

A team-level setting controlling how many recent completed sprints are used to calculate velocity. A shorter window reacts faster to recent performance; a longer window smooths out outlier sprints. Configured by Team Administrators and affects sprint capacity projections on the Sprint Calendar and Backlog.

## Also Known As

- Averaging Window
