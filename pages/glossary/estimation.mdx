# Estimation

The process of predicting the relative effort required to complete a story or bug, typically expressed in story points using a Fibonacci scale. Estimation is performed collaboratively by the development team — often using planning poker — to surface differing assumptions and reach a shared understanding. Estimates feed into sprint planning, velocity calculations, and projected completion dates. In Atono, estimation is captured as the story size field (XXS through XXL), which maps to story points used in burndown charts and total story point counts.
