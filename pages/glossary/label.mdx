# Label

A free-form tag applied to stories, bugs, or epics to support ad-hoc categorization and filtering beyond the built-in fields. Labels are scoped to a workspace and can be created inline when editing a work item. Multiple labels can be applied to a single item. Labels appear in filters and saved views, making it easy to surface cross-team or cross-epic groups of related work.
