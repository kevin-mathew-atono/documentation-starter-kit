# Sprint Goal

A single, concise objective the Scrum team commits to achieving by the end of a sprint, set during sprint planning. The sprint goal provides focus and helps the team prioritize decisions when scope changes mid-sprint. In Atono, the sprint goal is displayed at the top of the active sprint view and is included in sprint review and retrospective records.
