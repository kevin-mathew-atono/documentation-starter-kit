# Sprint Goal

A concise objective that the Scrum team commits to achieving by the end of a sprint. The sprint goal gives the sprint a unified purpose and helps the team make decisions when work needs to be adjusted mid-sprint. Having a sprint goal distinct from individual story completion encourages teams to think about outcomes rather than outputs.
