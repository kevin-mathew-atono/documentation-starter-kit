# Assumption

A stated belief held as true for planning or scoping purposes, pending full validation. Assumptions reduce uncertainty enough to make decisions but should be tracked and revisited as work progresses. If an assumption proves false, it may become a risk or require a change request. Assumptions are often documented in story descriptions or design decisions sections.
