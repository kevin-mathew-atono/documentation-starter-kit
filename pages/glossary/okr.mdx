# OKR

Objectives and Key Results, a goal-setting framework linking qualitative objectives (what to achieve) to measurable key results (how success is measured). OKRs are typically set at quarterly or annual cadences at the company, team, and individual level. Teams use OKRs to align agile delivery work with strategic outcomes, often mapping epics or product themes to specific key results.
