# Duplicate

A copy of an existing story or bug created with the same title (prefixed 'Copy of'), description, risk rating (bugs), and size/product theme (stories). Workflow step, assignee, backlog, and timebox handling varies by original item's category. Can optionally be linked to the original. Preserves team membership for items in To do/In Progress; unassigns items in Done/Won't do.
