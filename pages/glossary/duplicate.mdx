# Duplicate

An action that creates a copy of a story or bug, prefixing its title with 'Copy of' and carrying over the description, risk rating (bugs), and size/product theme (stories). The copy's workflow step, assignee, backlog position, and timebox depend on the original item's workflow category. Items in To do or In Progress keep their team assignment; items in Done or Won't do are unassigned. The duplicate can optionally be linked to the original.
