# Subtask

An implementation step within a story or bug with title, optional description (up to 1000 chars with rich text), assignee, and status (To do, In progress, Done, Won't do). Displayed with a progress meter excluding Won't do items. Changes are logged in Activities with dedicated filter. Can be reordered by dragging. Not available on drafts.
