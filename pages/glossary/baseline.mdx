# Baseline

A fixed reference snapshot of a project's original scope, estimates, or schedule used as a comparison point to measure change over time. Baselines help teams understand how much scope has grown (scope creep), whether cycle times are improving, and how well initial estimates held up. In planning discussions, 'baseline' often refers to the original sprint commitment before any additions or removals.
