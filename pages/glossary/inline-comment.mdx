# Inline Comment

A comment anchored to a specific word, phrase, or passage within a story's user story text, acceptance criteria, or additional content. Inline comments surface in context alongside the highlighted text, making it easy to discuss specific details without losing their location. Resolved inline comments are archived in Activities.

## Also Known As

- Anchored Comment
