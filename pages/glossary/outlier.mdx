# Outlier

A story or bug with unusually long cycle time, marked to exclude it from average cycle time calculations, cycle time reports, staleness indicators, and estimated completion dates. Outlier status can be added or removed by Workspace Owners, Administrators, Backlog Owners, and Team Administrators. Preserves the item's data for reference.
