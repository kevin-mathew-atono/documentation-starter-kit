# Incident

An unplanned interruption or significant reduction in the quality of a production service. Incidents range from complete outages to degraded performance and typically require immediate investigation and resolution. In Atono, incidents are tracked as bugs with a high risk rating, using the affected environment field to indicate the impacted deployment stage and the description to capture reproduction steps, impact scope, and timeline. A postmortem may follow to prevent recurrence.

## Also Known As

- Production Incident
- Outage
