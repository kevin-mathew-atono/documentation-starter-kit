# Sprint Backlog

The set of product backlog items selected for a sprint, combined with the team's plan for delivering them toward the sprint goal. The sprint backlog is owned by the development team and is updated daily as work progresses. Items can be added or removed with caution, and Atono warns when sprint scope is modified after the sprint starts. The sprint backlog is visible on the In Progress and Backlog pages during an active sprint.
