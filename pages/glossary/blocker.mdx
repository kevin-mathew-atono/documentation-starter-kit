# Blocker

An impediment preventing a story or bug from progressing through the workflow. Blockers can be internal (a missing decision, an unresolved dependency) or external (a third-party outage, waiting on another team). Identified in daily standups, blockers should be escalated and resolved by the team or Scrum Master as quickly as possible to minimize cycle time impact.
