# Team

A group of users organized to plan and deliver work together in Atono, using either Scrum or Kanban workflow. Each team has its own workflow, backlog, members, 1+ team admins, optional backlog owners, and can be public or private. Limited to 50 teams per workspace, 50 members per team. Has optional time zone setting for consistent reporting.
