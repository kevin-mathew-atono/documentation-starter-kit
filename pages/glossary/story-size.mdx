# Story Size

A relative measure of effort to complete a story: Unsized, XXS, XS, S, M, L, XL, XXL. Can be manually set or suggested by Atono based on similar completed stories (requires 9+ sized stories in 3+ sizes). Converted to story points for planning. Cleared when story is reassigned to a different team.
