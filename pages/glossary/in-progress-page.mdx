# In Progress (Page)

A team page showing active work moving through workflow steps. For Scrum teams, displays only the active sprint. For Kanban teams, shows the 20 most recent To do items plus all work in progress. Supports list/board views, filters, staleness indicators, and optional display of pull requests, product themes, timeboxes, and total story points.
