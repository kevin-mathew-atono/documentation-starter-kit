# Workflow

A customizable sequence of 4-25 steps that stories and bugs move through from ready-to-start to completion. Each team has its own workflow with steps organized into four categories (To do, In progress, Done, Won't do). Steps can be renamed, reordered, colored, added, deleted, and configured for applicability to stories/bugs. Time in In Progress steps contributes to cycle time.
