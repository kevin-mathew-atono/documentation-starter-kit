# Workflow

A customizable sequence of 4–25 named steps that stories and bugs move through from ready-to-start to completion. Each step belongs to one of four workflow categories: To do, In Progress, Done, or Won't do. Steps have a name and color. Only one step can exist in the To do category and one in the Won't do category; the In Progress and Done categories can have multiple steps. Cycle time is measured across all In Progress steps. Teams can reorder steps and rename them; the workflow is shared across all team members.
