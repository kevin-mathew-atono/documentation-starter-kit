# Kill Switch

A feature flag configured specifically to provide an immediate, reliable way to disable a feature across all users in any environment without a code deployment. Kill switches are a safety mechanism for production incidents, allowing teams to turn off a misbehaving feature in seconds. Best practice is to keep kill switches separate from flags used for gradual rollouts or A/B testing.

## Also Known As

- Emergency Disable
- Circuit Breaker Flag
