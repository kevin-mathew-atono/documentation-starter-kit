# Flow Efficiency

The ratio of active work time to total lead time for a work item, expressed as a percentage. Flow efficiency reveals how much of an item's lifespan is spent in active development versus waiting (in queue, blocked, or idle). High flow efficiency indicates minimal waste; low flow efficiency points to queuing or blocking problems. A team average above 15% is considered good in most software contexts.
