# Backlog (Page)

A team page for planning and prioritizing upcoming work before it moves into execution. For Scrum teams, organized around sprints with projected capacity. For Kanban teams, displays all 'To do' items beyond the 20 shown in In Progress. Supports list view with filters, bulk actions, and right-click quick actions.
