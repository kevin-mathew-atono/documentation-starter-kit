# Velocity

The average number of story points a Scrum team completes per sprint, calculated from completed sprints within the team's configured averaging window. Used to project capacity for future sprints and shown in sprint capacity bars on the Sprint Calendar and Backlog. Requires at least 2 completed sprints.
