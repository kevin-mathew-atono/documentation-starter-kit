# Velocity

The average number of story points a Scrum team completes per sprint, calculated from the team's most recent completed sprints within the configured averaging window. Teams with fewer completed sprints than the window use all available data. Velocity informs sprint planning by indicating how much work a team can realistically take on, and is used to project completion dates for backlog items.
