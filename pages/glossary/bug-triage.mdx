# Bug Triage

A workspace-wide process and page for evaluating and prioritizing reported bugs before assigning them to teams. Bugs remain in triage until assigned a team backlog and risk rating. Includes Reported Bugs, More Info Required, and Won't Do sections. Shows badge when bugs are older than 7 days.
