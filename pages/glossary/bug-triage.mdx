# Bug Triage

A workspace-wide page and process for evaluating newly reported bugs before they are assigned to a team. Bugs remain in triage until they receive a team assignment and a risk rating (calculated as probability × impact). The triage page is organized into Reported Bugs, More Info Required, and Won't Do sections. A badge indicates when bugs have been in triage for more than seven days without action.
