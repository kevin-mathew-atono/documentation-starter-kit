# Feature Request

A suggestion from a user, customer, or stakeholder for new or improved product functionality. Feature requests are the raw material of product discovery and are evaluated against user needs, strategic goals, and delivery capacity before being accepted into the backlog. In Atono, feature requests are typically captured as stories in Story Refinement, where they are refined, sized, and prioritized before being assigned to a team backlog and sprint.
