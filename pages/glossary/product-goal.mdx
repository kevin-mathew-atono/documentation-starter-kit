# Product Goal

A long-term, outcome-oriented objective that provides direction for the product team's work across multiple sprints or releases. The product goal describes a future state of the product that delivers value to users or the business. In Scrum, the product goal is the current strategic objective the product backlog serves. Teams often align product themes and epics with active product goals.
