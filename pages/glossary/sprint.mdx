# Sprint

A time-boxed iteration for Scrum teams, typically 1-4 weeks, with defined start/end dates and committed scope. Sprints cannot overlap for the same team. Active sprints appear in In Progress; future sprints are planned in Backlog. Includes capacity projection based on velocity, burndown charts, and total story points tracking. Created, edited, and deleted by backlog owners if Backlog Management is enabled.
