# Acceptance Test

A test that validates a story meets its acceptance criteria from the user's perspective. Acceptance tests are typically written alongside acceptance criteria and run before a story is marked as done. They may be manual or automated and are distinct from unit or integration tests, focusing on user-observable behavior rather than internal implementation.
