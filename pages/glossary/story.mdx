# Story

A unit of user-focused work including title, one or more user stories, hierarchical acceptance criteria, and optional additional content. Moves through team workflows, can be assigned, sized (XXS-XXL), linked to epics/product themes/timeboxes, and tracked with cycle time. Supports feature flags, comments, attachments, version history, duplication, splitting, and subtasks. Sized stories contribute to total story points.
