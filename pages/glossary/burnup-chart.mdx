# Burnup Chart

A chart showing two lines over time: total work scope and total work completed. Unlike a burndown chart, which shows remaining work, a burnup chart makes scope changes visible as upward movement in the scope line. Burnup charts are useful when scope is expected to change during a sprint or release, as they clearly distinguish between work added and work completed.
