# Product Backlog

An ordered list of all work known to be needed in the product, maintained and prioritized by the product owner. The product backlog is never complete — it evolves as the product and its context change. Higher-priority items are more refined and ready to work on; lower-priority items may be rough ideas. In Atono, the product backlog is managed through a combination of the team Backlog page and Story Refinement, where ideas are captured and prepared before being committed to a team.
