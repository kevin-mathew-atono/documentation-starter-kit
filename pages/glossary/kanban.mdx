# Kanban

A team workflow type focused on continuous flow rather than time-boxed iterations. Kanban teams pull work from a 'To do' queue into active workflow steps, aiming to minimize work in progress and maximize throughput. Estimated completion dates are calculated from average cycle time and backlog queue position. Kanban teams do not have sprints, velocity, or burndown charts.
