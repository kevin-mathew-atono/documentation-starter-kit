# Risk Rating

A numerical assessment of a bug's overall risk, calculated by multiplying probability (1-5) by impact (1-5) to produce a value from 1-25, where 25 is highest risk. Can be manually set or suggested by Atono based on bug description. Required along with team assignment for a bug to move out of Bug Triage.
