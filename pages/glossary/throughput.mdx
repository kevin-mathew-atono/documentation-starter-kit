# Throughput

The number of work items a team completes per unit of time, regardless of their size or complexity. Throughput is a flow-based metric often used alongside cycle time to understand team delivery rate. Unlike velocity, throughput does not weight items by story points, making it useful for teams that prefer not to estimate. In Kanban, throughput is a primary indicator of team performance.
