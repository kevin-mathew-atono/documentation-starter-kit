# Technical Debt

Accumulated shortcuts, deferred improvements, and suboptimal design decisions in a codebase that require future rework. Technical debt may be intentional (a deliberate shortcut to meet a deadline) or unintentional (poor design discovered later). Left unaddressed, technical debt slows future development and increases the risk of bugs. Teams often track technical debt as stories or bugs with a dedicated product theme.
