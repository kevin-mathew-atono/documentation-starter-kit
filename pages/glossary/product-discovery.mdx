# Product Discovery

The process of identifying, validating, and scoping user problems before committing to building a solution. Discovery activities include user interviews, prototype testing, data analysis, and competitive research. Discovery outputs—such as validated problem statements, personas, and feature hypotheses—feed directly into story authoring, acceptance criteria, and product themes in Atono.
