# Rollout

A controlled, gradual release of a feature to an increasing subset of users, customers, or environments. Rollouts reduce the risk of a full release by allowing teams to monitor behavior and catch issues before exposing a feature to all users. In Atono, rollouts are implemented by configuring feature flag slices to target specific customers or locations and expanding the targeting over time.

## Also Known As

- Gradual Rollout
- Phased Release
