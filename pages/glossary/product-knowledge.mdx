# Product Knowledge

A feature that analyzes help documentation and builds a structured glossary of up to 200 key product concepts including name, description, synonyms, and relationships. Exported as TSV for use with AI tools. Managed by Glossary Contributors and Owners with permissions, version history, and conflict detection.
