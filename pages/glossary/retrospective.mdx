# Retrospective

A recurring ceremony held at the end of each sprint where the Scrum team reflects on how they worked together and identifies specific improvements to carry into the next sprint. Typical formats ask what went well, what could be improved, and what actions to take. Retrospectives are a core Scrum event focused on continuous team improvement rather than product output.
