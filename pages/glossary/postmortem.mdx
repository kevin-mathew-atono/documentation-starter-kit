# Postmortem

A structured retrospective analysis conducted after a production incident to understand what went wrong, why it happened, and how to prevent recurrence. Blameless postmortems focus on systemic causes rather than individual mistakes and produce concrete action items such as new tests, monitoring improvements, or process changes. In Atono, postmortem action items are typically captured as bugs or technical investigation stories linked to the original incident bug.

## Also Known As

- Post-Incident Review
- Blameless Postmortem
- Root Cause Analysis
