# Estimated Completion Date

A forecast for when items in a Kanban team's To do category will be completed, based on the team's average cycle time and the current backlog queue order. Updates dynamically as items are reordered. Highlighted in orange when falling after an item's timebox end date. Not available for Scrum teams or outliers.
