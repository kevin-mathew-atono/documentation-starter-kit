# Portfolio

A collection of epics, product themes, or strategic initiatives representing a major investment area for the organization. Portfolio management involves prioritizing and sequencing these larger units of work to allocate team capacity toward the highest-value opportunities. In Atono, portfolios are approximated through product themes and timeline views spanning multiple teams.
