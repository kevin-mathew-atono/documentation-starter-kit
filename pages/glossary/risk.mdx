# Risk

A potential future event or condition that could negatively affect the delivery or quality of a story, sprint, or release. Risks differ from issues in that they have not yet occurred. Teams commonly identify and track risks during sprint planning or retrospectives. In Atono, bug risk is quantified as a risk rating (probability × impact), but broader project risks are typically discussed in team meetings or tracked in story descriptions.
