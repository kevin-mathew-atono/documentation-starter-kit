# Story Status

The current position of a story within a team's workflow, represented by a workflow step such as To Do, In Progress, In Review, or Done. Story status determines which workflow category the item falls into and affects visibility on boards, burndown charts, and velocity calculations. Status changes are recorded in the Activities log with a timestamp and the name of the user who made the change.
