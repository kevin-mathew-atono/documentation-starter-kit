# Active Sprint

The single sprint a Scrum team is currently executing. Only one sprint can be active per team at a time. Items in the active sprint appear on the In Progress page and contribute to burndown charts and velocity calculations when the sprint closes. A new sprint cannot be started until the current one is completed or cancelled.
