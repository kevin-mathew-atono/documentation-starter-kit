# Scope

The defined set of features, stories, or work committed to for a sprint, epic, release, or project. Scope sets expectations for what will and will not be delivered in a given timeframe. Scope changes mid-sprint are discouraged in Scrum; Atono warns teams before allowing sprint scope modifications. Scope is often discussed alongside capacity to determine whether commitments are realistic.

## Also Known As

- Project Scope
