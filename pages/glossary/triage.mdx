# Triage

The process of reviewing newly created bugs or issues to assess their severity, priority, and validity before they enter active development. During triage a team member verifies the report, assigns a severity level, sets a priority, and routes the item to the appropriate team or backlog. In Atono, bug triage is supported by severity and priority fields, bulk editing, and filtered views that surface unreviewed items.
