# Data Export

The process of downloading workspace data in a portable format for external analysis, archiving, or migration. Data exports may include stories, bugs, epics, comments, and workflow history. Exports are typically available in CSV or JSON format and are accessible to Workspace Owners and Administrators from workspace settings.
