# Burndown Chart

A Scrum team chart tracking how many story points remain in a sprint over time. Compares actual progress against an ideal burn rate, projects when the sprint will finish based on recent velocity, and flags items at risk of not completing or available to pull in. Accessible for active and completed sprints only. Warns before sprint scope is modified. Uses the team's time zone for date grouping.
