# Burndown Chart

A sprint-level chart for Scrum teams showing story points remaining over time compared to an ideal guideline. Displays actual progress, projected completion based on recent velocity, and highlights items at risk or available to pull in. Includes warnings before modifying sprint scope. Available for active and completed sprints but not future sprints. Uses team time zone for date grouping.
