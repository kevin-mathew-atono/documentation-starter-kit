# Burndown Chart

A Scrum team chart tracking remaining story points in a sprint over time, updated as stories are completed or moved out of scope. Plots actual remaining work against an ideal linear burn rate and projects sprint completion based on recent team velocity. Flags stories at risk of not finishing and surfaces items that could be pulled in if capacity allows. Available for active and completed sprints; the team's configured time zone is used for date grouping.
