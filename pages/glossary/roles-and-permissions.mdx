# Roles and Permissions

Atono's multi-level access control system including workspace roles (Workspace Owner, Administrator, Product Manager, Standard User), team roles (Team Member, Team Administrator, Backlog Owner), and separate feature flag and glossary permissions. Roles can stack to grant combined privileges.
