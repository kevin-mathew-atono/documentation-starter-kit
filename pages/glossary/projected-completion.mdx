# Projected Completion

An estimated date when a story or bug will be completed, calculated differently for Kanban and Scrum teams. For Kanban teams in To do or In progress, based on current position, team cycle time, and daily throughput. For Scrum teams, reflects sprint timing (unscheduled or sprint end date). Warnings appear when projected date falls after timebox end date.
