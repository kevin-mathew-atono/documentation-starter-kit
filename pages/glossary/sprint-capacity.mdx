# Sprint Capacity

The total amount of work, measured in story points, that a team plans to complete in a sprint based on available team members, their working hours, and any planned time off. Sprint capacity is set during sprint planning and compared against the sum of story points in the sprint backlog to avoid overcommitment. A team's historical velocity is the primary input for setting a realistic capacity target.
