# Story Refinement

A workspace-wide backlog for capturing ideas and preparing stories before assigning them to teams. Has fixed workflow steps (Idea, Refinement, Ready for Assignment, Won't Do) accessible to all users. Does not track sizing, estimated completion dates, cycle time, or staleness.
