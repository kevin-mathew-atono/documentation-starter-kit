# Parking Lot

A temporary holding area for ideas, topics, or work items that are raised during a meeting or planning session but are not immediately actionable or in scope. Items in the parking lot are noted for future consideration rather than ignored. In Atono, the parking lot concept is often implemented using the Won't do workflow category or Story Refinement for ideas not yet ready to be committed.

## Also Known As

- Idea Backlog
