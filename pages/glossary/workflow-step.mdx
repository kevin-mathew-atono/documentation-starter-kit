# Workflow Step

A named stage in a workflow with assigned color and category (To do, In progress, Done, Won't do). Can be configured to apply to stories, bugs, or both. Steps can be reordered, renamed, added (up to 25 total), deleted (with reassignment), and moved between categories by team admins and workspace Administrators.
