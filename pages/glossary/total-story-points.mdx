# Total Story Points

The combined Fibonacci-based size of all visible stories in a column, sprint, or timebox, including an estimated size for unsized stories based on team average. Appears on Scrum backlogs (sprint headers), In Progress pages (workflow steps), Sprint Calendar (future sprints with projected capacity), Everything page (when filtered to one team), and timelines/timeboxes (when all items belong to one team). Updates dynamically as stories are added, removed, or resized.
