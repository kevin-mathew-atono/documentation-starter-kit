# Attachment

A file added to a story or bug, displayed as thumbnails for images/videos or as file info for other types. Can be attached inline to specific acceptance criteria or to the general attachments area. Limited to 100 files per item, 50 MB per file, with workspace-level storage limits based on plan. Attachment URLs expire after a short time and must be re-fetched.
