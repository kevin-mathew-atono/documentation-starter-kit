# Split

An action dividing a story into two stories by moving selected acceptance criteria into a new story. Preserves team, product theme, timebox, and feature flags on both stories. AC parents may be moved or copied based on children selection. Both stories are automatically linked. Available only to users with appropriate permissions on managed backlogs.
