# Sprint Calendar

A Scrum-only team page providing a visual schedule of past, active, and future sprints. Shows sprint names, item counts, story point totals, and projected capacity for future sprints. Supports scrolling, zooming (days to 5 years), resizing sprint height, creating/editing/deleting sprints, and viewing sprint details including burndown charts.
