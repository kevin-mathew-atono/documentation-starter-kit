# Product Manager

A workspace role controlling who can manage product themes. When assigned in a workspace, Product Managers can add/remove themes, manage backlog owners for any team, access all public and private team backlogs, and manage glossary concepts. If no Product Managers are assigned, any user can manage product themes.
