# Ask Capy

An AI-powered Q&amp;A tool that searches completed stories and bugs in your workspace to answer product questions. Requires at least 10 completed items to activate. Each workspace gets 50 questions per day; the counter resets at midnight Eastern time. Every answer includes the source items it was drawn from.
