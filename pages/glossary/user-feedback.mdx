# User Feedback

Input gathered from users through interviews, surveys, support tickets, usage analytics, or direct observation that reveals how people interact with the product and what problems they experience. User feedback drives feature requests, informs acceptance criteria, and validates product decisions. In Atono, insights from user feedback are typically documented in story descriptions or design decisions sections and may be linked to customer data visible through feature engagement and usage tracking.

## Also Known As

- Customer Feedback
- Voice of the Customer
