# Search

A fuzzy, keyword-based search across story, bug, and epic titles, IDs, descriptions, user stories, acceptance criteria, and active comments. Accessible via Cmd+K (Mac) or Ctrl+K (Windows). Results update as you type, support filters, and include snippets showing match location. Saves the 10 most recent searches with result counts.
