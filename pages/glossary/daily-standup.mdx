# Daily Standup

A short, time-boxed synchronization meeting held each working day where team members share what they completed yesterday, what they plan to do today, and any blockers preventing progress. Also called a daily scrum in the Scrum framework. The standup is designed to improve team coordination and surface impediments early, not to provide status reports to management.
