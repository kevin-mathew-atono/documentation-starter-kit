# Filter

Criteria applied to narrow visible items in backlogs, the Everything page, timelines, or reports. Filters are multi-select (items matching any value are included) and stackable (items must match all filters). Common filters include team, workflow step, assignee, timebox, product theme, risk rating, story size, created/updated/completed dates, and external ID.
