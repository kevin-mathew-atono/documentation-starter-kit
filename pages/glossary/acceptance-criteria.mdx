# Acceptance Criteria

Testable conditions that define when a story is complete, written as a hierarchical list with up to three levels of nesting. Also called ACs. Each top-level criterion can have sub-criteria and leaf-level criteria. Criteria support inline comments anchored to specific text, attachments, and persistent URLs. When a story is split, acceptance criteria are preserved based on whether parent criteria are moved with all their children. Acceptance criteria are versioned and any edit appears in the story's Activities log.
