# Acceptance Criteria

Testable conditions that define when a story is complete, written as a hierarchical list of requirements. Also referred to as ACs. Each criterion can have inline comments, attachments, and a persistent URL. Acceptance criteria are preserved during story splits based on whether parent criteria are moved with all their children.
