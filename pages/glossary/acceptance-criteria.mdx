# Acceptance Criteria

Testable conditions that define when a story is complete, written as a hierarchical list with up to three levels of nesting. Also called ACs. Criteria can have sub-criteria and leaf-level criteria, support attachments, and link to persistent URLs. Any edit to acceptance criteria is versioned and recorded in the story's Activities log. When a story is split, criteria are preserved based on whether their parent criteria move with them.
