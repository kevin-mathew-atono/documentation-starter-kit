# Product Owner

The Scrum role responsible for maximizing the value of the product by managing, ordering, and communicating the contents of the product backlog. The product owner is the single accountable person for backlog priority decisions and ensures the team always works on the highest-value items. In Atono, the product owner's responsibilities are typically held by a user with the Backlog Owner team role and the Product Manager workspace role, giving them control over backlog ordering, sprint planning, and product themes.
